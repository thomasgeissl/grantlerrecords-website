13/ 14

  • Midwife - Forever

    2020/05/10
    already a month old, but still worth sharing. Denver musician Madeline Johnston released her second album, she calls her music heaven metal.
    Forever was released through The Flenser on April 10, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Mark Lanegan - Straight Songs Of Sorrow

    2020/05/08
    The former Screaming Trees frontman. Queens of the Stone Age collaborator and friend of Kurt Cobain released his 12th solo album, Straight Songs Of Sorrow. his book Sing Backwards and Weep was released last week. he has clearly seen and experienced some shit in his life.
    Straight Songs Of Sorrow was released through Heavenly Recordings on May 8, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Field Works - Ultrasonic

    2020/05/03
    Stuart Hyatt, echolocations of bats, nature and many contributors.
    Ultrasonic was released on Temporary Residence on May 1, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Ride, Pêtr Aleksänder - Clouds In The Mirror

    2020/05/01
    London-based duo Pêtr Aleksänder (Tom Hobden, Noah & The Whale and Eliot James, Two Door Cinema Club) transformed Ride's album This Is Not A Safe Place into a new athmospheric piece.
    Clouds In The Mirrow was released on Wichita Recordings on May 1, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Shards & Isolation Choir - Inside I'll Sing

    2020/04/26
    London-based experimental vocal ensemble Shards teamed up with a couple of musicians and released Inside I'll Sing. their Isolation Choir consists of Douglas Dare, Joe Newman (Alt-J), Jonathan Donahue (Mercury Rev), Anna Meredith, New York-based composer Nico Muhly, Luke Howard, Hatis Noit and many more. proceeds will be donated.
    Inside I'll Sing was released on Erased Tapes on April 17, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Other Lives - For Their Love

    2020/04/24
    having experimented with electronic textues on their last album (Rituals, 2015), Other Lives went back to an analog setup for the new album. it was recorded in songwriter Jesse Tabish' new house somewhere in the rural Cooper Mountain region of Oregon.
    For Their Love was released on ATO Records on April 24, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Die Wilde Jagd - Haut

    2020/04/24
    4 songs, 45 minutes.
    Haut was released on Bureau B on April 17, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Slut - For The Soul There Is No Hospital

    2020/04/18
    after seven silent years, Slut just released their new single For The Soul There Is No Hospital.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Chromatics - Teacher

    2020/04/18
    Chromatics have released their new song Teacher and the tracklist for their upcoming album Dear Tommy. a few weeks ago they already released Faded Now, a suprise album which includes a few remixes of songs from Closer To Grey and a couple of new ones.
    Teacher was released through Italians Do It Better on April 17, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Soap&Skin - What's up?

    2020/04/14
    And another very beautiful cover by Anja Plaschg, aka Soap&Skin. she and her sister Evelyn just published a new interpretation of the 90s classic What's up by the 4 Non Blondes. and I pray, oh my god do I pray, I pray every single day, for a revolution.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.
