9/ 14

  • Angel Olsen - Whole New Mess

    2020/08/28
    Angel Olsen released a raw and more intimate version of her All Mirrors lp.
    Whole New Mess was released on Jagjaguwar on August 28, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify, bandcamp or somewhere else.

  • For Belarus - For Belarus

    2020/08/26
    a new compilation, For Belarus, has been released to support the victims of repression and police violence in Belarus. the funds are being sent directly to the Belarus Solidarity Foundation – one of several charitable organisations currently helping the country. it includes tracks by Molly Nilsson, Thomas Azier, Molchat Doma and many more.
    Enjoy it on bandcamp.

  • Bright Eyes - Down in the Weeds, Where the World Once Was

    2020/08/19
    Bright Eyes return with their first new album in nine years.
    Down in the Weeds, Where the World Once Was was released through Dead Oceans on August 21, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Library Tapes - The Quiet City

    2020/08/21
    David Wenngren released a new Library Tapes album, it was made in collaboration with many other artists. it contains 10 instrumental tracks.
    The Quite City was released on his label 1631 Recordings on August 21, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • The Notwist - Ship

    2020/08/21
    The Notwist released a new ep.
    Ship was released on Morr Music on August 21, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Meena - EP

    2020/08/19
    Manchester based shoegaze duo Meena released their debut ep.
    EP was self-released in July 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Black Marble - I Must Be Living Twice

    2020/08/14
    Brooklyn based Chris Stewart release a cover ep, including songs by Wire, Robert Palmer, Grouper and more.
    I Must Be Living Twice was released on Sacred Bones Records on August 14, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Sin Fang - The Last Shall Be First

    2020/08/14
    Reykjavík based Sindri Már Sigfússon (Seabear) released a new album.
    The Last Shall Be First was released on August 14, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify.

  • Land of Talk - Indistinct Conversations

    2020/08/07
    Land of Talk, the band around lead singer Elizabeth Powell released their fourth album.
    Indistinct Conversations was released on Saddle Creek Records on July 31, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Alison Mosshart - Sound Wheel

    2020/08/07
    Alison Mosshart (The Kills) released her first solo spoken word album, it contains 47 short tracks and it is a companion album to her new book Car Ma.
    Sound Wheel was released on Third Man Store on Agust 7, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.
