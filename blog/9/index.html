  • Damon Albarn - The Nearer The Fountain, More Pure The Stream Flows

    2021/11/16
    Damon Albarn released his second solo album. it was intended as an orchestral piece, plans changed as the covid 19 pandemic made him cancel his tour. he said in a statement: I have been on my own dark journey while making this record and it led me to believe that a pure source might still exist.
    The Nearer The Fountain, More Pure The Stream Flows was released on Transgressive on November 12, 2021.
    Enjoy it on spotify, youtube or somewhere elsee.
