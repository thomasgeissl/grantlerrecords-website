  • Ja, Panik - Die Gruppe

    2021/4/30
    we are lucky that Andreas Spechtl, the most sophisticated DEnglish singer since Falco after some also very promising solo projects finally decided to release another album with his group Ja, Panik again. the dystopian songs maybe even fit a bit too well to the current situation. although they were all written before it. they are also accompanied by a modular synthesizer and a saxophone now. and they even manage to let that sound neither jazzy nor cheesy 80s careless whisperish. with that they proof that there is still some beauty left in this world. even if it’s only within.
    Die Gruppe was released on Buereau B on April 30, 2021.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.
