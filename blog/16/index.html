  • Sigur Rós – Odin’s Raven Magic

    2020/12/04
    finally!
    Odin’s Raven Magic is a collaboration between Sigur Rós, Hilmar Örn Hilmarsson, Steindór Andersen and Maria Huld Markan Sigfúsdóttir (Amiina). it was originally premiered during 2002 at London's Barbican centre.
    Odin’s Raven Magic was released on Krunk on Dezember 4, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify, bandcamp or somewhere else.

  • Girls in Airports - Dive

    2020/12/02
    Girls in Airports released their sixth studio album. they create beautiful soundscapes by combining jazz, indie and urban folk.
    Dive was released on Mawi Music on November 27, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Chilly Gonzales - ENYA: A Treatise on Unguilty Pleasures

    2020/11/28
    Chilly Gonzales released a book about Enya, taste, authenticity. it is available as an audio book as well.
    Enjoy it on spotify or buy it here.

  • Ane Brun - How Beauty Holds The Hand Of Sorrow

    2020/11/27
    Norwegian musician Ane Brun released her eighth album. it is quite different to its predecessor After The Great Strom, which was released last month.
    How Beauty Holds The Hand Of Sorrow was released on Balloon Ranger Recordings on November 27, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Clara Luzia - 4 + 1

  • Silent Sex - Ohnmacht

    2020/11/20
    based in minga. and very much into c major and reverb. the new silent sex single is available on most major streaming services. don't hesitate to message us for stems, if you want to sample, remix or be otherwise creative with it.
    Ohnmacht was released on Grantler Records on November 20, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify.

  • Молчат Дома - Дискотека

  • The Goalie's Anxiety at the Penalty Kick - Ways of Hearing

    2020/11/07
    taking their name from a Peter Handke's novel, adapted into Wim Wenders's first feature, The Goalie's Anxiety at the Penalty Kick is a new Philadelphia based band. their debut Ways of Hearing reminds of early Bright Eyes records.
    Ways of Hearing was released on Count Your Lucky Stars on October 30, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.
