  • Slut - For The Soul There Is No Hospital

    2020/04/18
    after seven silent years, Slut just released their new single For The Soul There Is No Hospital.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Chromatics - Teacher

    2020/04/18
    Chromatics have released their new song Teacher and the tracklist for their upcoming album Dear Tommy. a few weeks ago they already released Faded Now, a suprise album which includes a few remixes of songs from Closer To Grey and a couple of new ones.
    Teacher was released through Italians Do It Better on April 17, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Soap&Skin - What's up?

    2020/04/14
    And another very beautiful cover by Anja Plaschg, aka Soap&Skin. she and her sister Evelyn just published a new interpretation of the 90s classic What's up by the 4 Non Blondes. and I pray, oh my god do I pray, I pray every single day, for a revolution.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Porridge Radio - Every Bad

    2020/04/09
    "grantig", repetitive and from Brighton.
    Every Bad was released on Secretly Canadian on March 13th, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • M. Ward - Migration Stories

    2020/04/04
    Migration Stories is M. Ward's (She & Him, Monsters of Folk) tenth studio release and was recorded at Arcarde Fire's Montreal studio.
    it is his debut for ANTI records and was realeased on April 4, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Social Isolation

    2020/04/03
    Created and compiled during the last week of March 2020, this compilation brings together the sound of artists in isolation creating music that's reflective of our collective struggle during the Covid-19 pandemic. Those featured in this edition are of Indian origin [src].
    Enjoy it on bandcamp.

  • Daniel Avery, Alessandro Cortini - Illusion of Time

    2020/04/02
    yet another dreamy and atmospheric new album. Daniel Avery and Alessandro Cortini (Nine Inch Nails) joined forces and remotely made an album full of distorted drones, warm synth pads, feedback loops and other weird noises.
    Illusion of Time was released through Phantasy Sounds on March 27, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify.

  • Pantha Du Prince - Conference of Trees

    2020/04/02
    already a month ago, the German musician Hendrik Weber aka Pantha Du Prince released the new album Conference of Trees. it has been performed at several theatre gigs last year already. what does it sound like when trees communicate?
    Conference of Threes was released through BMG's Modern Recordings on March 6, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Nine Inch Nails - Ghosts V-VI

    2020/04/01
    Nine Inch Nails released volumes five and six of their Ghosts series, subtitled Together and Locusts respectively. these two new releases are available for free on their website.
    Ghosts V and VI were released on their label The Null Corporation on March 26, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Muzz - Broken Tambourine

    2020/03/31
    Muzz was formed by Paul Banks (Interpol), Matt Barrick (the Walkmen, Fleet Foxes) and Josh Kaufman (Bonny Light Horseman, Day of the Dead). after dropping their first track Bad Feeling on SoundCloud earlier this month, the band now officially released their first single.
    Broken Tambourine was released on Matador Records on March 24, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.
