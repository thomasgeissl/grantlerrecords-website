  • Widowspeak - Honeychurch

    2021/1/22

  • Grandbrothers - All the Unknown

    2021/1/15
    Erol Sarp and Lukas Vogel are back with their third lp. as on previous albums, All the Unknown fuses piano sounds and electronics in a beautiful and elegant way. their making of video impressively shows the gear they use to create these warm sounds.
    All the Unknown was released through City Slang on January 15, 2021.
    Enjoy it on spotify, bandcamp or somewhere else.

  • Medio Mutante - 800%

    2020/12/20
    800% is a slow motion remix album of 13 Medio Mutante tracks from the last 13 years. It's over three hours long, it was remixed utilizing digital and analog tools such as microcassette recorders, paulstretch, mammut, max/msp and reel-to-reel tape machines.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.
