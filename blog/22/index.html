  • Sonic Boom - All Things Being Equal

    2020/06/05
    Pete Kember's new album sounds like a mix of Kraftwerk and Animal Collective. it is the first Sonic Boom album after Spectrum, which was released in 1989. in the meantime, the former Spacemen 3 member produced and collaborated with bands like MGMT, Beach House or Panda Bear and released music as Spectrum or E.A.R. (Experimental Audio Research).
    All Things Being Equal was released on Carpark Records on June 6, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or bandcamp.

  • Owen Pallett - Island

    2020/06/01
    Owen Pallett surprises with Island, his first studio album in 6 years. Island was recorded live at Abbey Road Studios with the London Contemporary Orchestra.
    Island was released on Domino on May 22, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify, youtube or somewhere else.

  • Nation of Language - Introduction, Presence

    2020/05/31
    Brooklyn based new wave trio Nation of Language self-released their debut Introduction, Presence.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Christian Lee Hutson - Beginners

    2020/05/30
    LA based singer songwriter Christian Lee Hutson released his new album Beginners. it contains ten honest and intimate tracks. it was produced by Phoebe Bridgers (Boygenius and Better Oblivion Community Center).
    Beginners was released on ANTI records on May 29, 2020.
    Enjoy it on spotify or youtube.

  • Cigarettes After Sex - You're All I Want

    Enjoy it on spotify.
